About Us

Our Story

MediTrack was created to simplify medication management and improve health outcomes. Our team recognized the challenges of remembering medications, especially for seniors and those with chronic conditions. To solve this, we built an intuitive app that provides smart reminders, refill alerts, and seamless tracking to ensure no dose is missed.
